<p class="bc">CAMBRIA COUNTY BIRTH INDEX<br>(Births Prior to 1906)
</p><p>
<table width="90%" align="center" border="0" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0">
  <tbody><tr><td class="b">
A Note of Caution:  It appears that, in some of these records, the spelling of names was "creative".  Fields were left blank.  The same birth could be recorded twice (possible, once by the parents and once by the doctor?) with slightly different information in each recording.  If births occurred in one year and were filed in another, the birth year could be recorded wrong.
