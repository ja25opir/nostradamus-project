<p></p><h2 id="cnsPageTitle">Beaufort County Government's General Meeting Agenda: Monday, July 12, 2010.</h2>
<div id="postStats">
	Author: <a href="http://beaufortcountynow.com/profile/admin">Stan Deatherage</a> | 	Published: <a href="http://beaufortcountynow.com/archive/2010-07-08/Thursday-July-8-2010.html">July 8th, 2010</a>
			</div>
			<br>
			<br>
			<div id="postContent">
<b>The Beaufort County Board of County Commissioners Will Meet to Discuss the County's Business </b>
<br>
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Government of Beaufort County meets every month for at least one General Meeting to discuss and act on the county's business.  Beaufort County employs the County Manager Form of Government, which stipulates that the board of county commissioners allow the county manager to set the agenda, and bring issues before them.   
<br>
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; This meeting is the first meeting after the conclusion of the county's budget process, in which the county lowered the tax rate to .50 per 100.00 valuation.  The most important item to be discussed in the July 12, 2010 general meeting occupies the third and sixth items on the agenda: The Beaufort County Medical Center.  It is the single largest issue that will affect Beaufort County this year, and the county commissioners must deal with it now, and in the future until it is resolved.  
<br>
<br>    
&nbsp;<b>Beaufort County Board of County Commissioners General Meeting Agenda</b>
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Monday, July 12, 2010
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>Beaufort County Administrative Building, Commissioners’ Meeting Room</b>
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;121 West Third Street, Washington, North Carolina
<br>
<br>
5:00 PM: Call To Order, Consideration of Consent Agenda 
<br>
<br>
5:20 PM: Public Comments
<br>
<br> 
5:30 PM: Don McNeil, Dixon-Hughes Auditing Firm
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Beaufort County Hospital Audit
<br>
<br>
5:45 PM: Marisol Barr, Martin County Community Action
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Update on Weatherization Program
<br>
<br>
6:00 PM: Earl Carney
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tax Revaluation &amp; Board of Equalization Informal Hearings
<br>
<br>
6:15 PM: Break
<br>
<br>
7:00 PM: Public Hearing – Installment Purchase Financing of Beaufort Regional Health System Real Estate 
<br>
<br>
7:20 PM: Commissioner Robert Cayton
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Letter of Appreciation to Eddy Davis Family
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Governor’s Appointment to NC Transportation Board At-Large Seat (Rural Eastern NC) 
<br>
<br>
7:30 PM: County Manager Reports</div>
