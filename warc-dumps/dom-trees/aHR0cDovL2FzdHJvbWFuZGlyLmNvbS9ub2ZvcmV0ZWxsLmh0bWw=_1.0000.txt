												<br>
												<br>
												Every weekend everybody looks up 
												STARS FORETELL column in 
												newspapers and periodicals, and 
												everybody grumbles that it does 
												not come true, or its very 
												vague. But its not at all 
												surprising, if you know that you 
												are one amongst trillions born 
												under that sunsign, which is 
												used to predict the future, in 
												these columns. The sun stays in 
												one sign for full one month, and 
												repeats year after year, ever 
												since the universe was formed. 
												Just calculate how many, all 
												over the world, take birth under 
												any particular sign. They all 
												cannot have the same fate, and 
												its rare chance that the learned 
												astrologer preparing the week’s 
												chart, takes your natal chart as 
												model. And, that only can tell 
												whether sun is in a malefic 
												house, or benefic one; and if 
												the lord of that house is 
												malefic or benefic.<br>
												<br>
												Another system that astrologers 
												use is by rashi , i.e. moonsign. 
												Moon stays in one sign for 2¼ 
												days, but repeats 28th day. That 
												means, moon is in the same sign 
												for 30 days in a year, and 
												prediction suffers same handicap 
												as in case of sunsign. In 
												personal natal chart, though, it 
												is more accurate because degrees 
												change faster and sets you apart 
												from other people born in the 
												same sign.<br>
												<br>
												The only way your future with 
												reasonable accuracy can be 
												predicted, is from your natal 
												chart, or janma kundli. It is 
												based on lagna or ascendant. 
												Lagna stays in one sign for 
												approximately two hours and 
												repeats every day. Thus, here 
												too are mind boggling number of 
												natives that are born under the 
												same lagna every year, and year 
												after year. But from the natal 
												chart prepared from lagna, a 
												learned astrologer knows where 
												each planet is placed and what 
												are the aspects and influences 
												on it. In addition, it tells the 
												degrees of the houses and the 
												planets, ascertaining their 
												strength and weakness, as well 
												as, if by virtue of occupancy, 
												or lordship of certain house, it 
												is malefic or benefic. Most 
												important are the degrees of 
												moon in a particular sign of the 
												natal chart, from which are 
												calculated the dasa or period.<br>
												<br>
												In the natal chart, there are 
												six benefic houses and six are 
												malefic; there are five benefic 
												planets and four malefic. They 
												all play a vital role in 
												determining the future of the 
												native. A benefic planet placed 
												in a malefic house will not give 
												that good result and vice-versa. 
												The lordship of the house too is 
												an important factor. Except Sun 
												and Moon, which own only one 
												house, Rahu and Ketu own none, 
												all other planets own two houses 
												each; and their weakness or 
												strength affect the life and 
												future, during their mahadasas 
												and antar, pratyantar (sub and 
												sub-sub periods).<br>
												<br>
												So, the only way to know your, 
												and yours, life and future is to 
												have your native chart cast, 
												with degrees of planets and 
												houses, and dasas calculated. 
												Now computers do it in a jiffy, 
												at a reasonable cost. Then a 
												good astrologer, will foretell 
												your personalized future, with 
												reasonable accuracy. STARS WILL 
												FORETELL !.<br>
