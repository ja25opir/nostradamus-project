<h2>Jenna and Dan are Married!!</h2>
<p>Last January, James and I were driving together in the morning, which is a rare occurrence, and we were listening to our&nbsp;<a href="http://q100atlanta.com/" target="_blank">favorite morning show</a> who was had started a segment called Tradio. Right after Christmas they had listeners call in and trade things that they had that they didn’t want for something they did want that maybe had a little less value. It’s Is kinda hard to explain, but this morning they had some jewelry up for trade, a necklace and a ring. James called in and offered a wedding package with us, and they chose our offer over the other options. There were a few offers to get our package, snake skin boots, a few pageant dresses, I can’t remember the others, but finally Jenna called up and offered a flat screen tv and a Wii for our photography! So, needless to say that was an amazing offer, and they took her up on it. Thank goodness! We met Jenna and Dan last March for some&nbsp;<a href="http://adfotoblog.net/2009/03/jenna-and-dan-are-engaged/" target="_blank">engagement pictures on the Marrietta Square</a>. Immediately I was so thankful that we got this rock star couple, with such a unique vision for their wedding, you will see what I mean.</p>
<p>Jenna and Dan chose an Irish Tavern in Buford, GA as the setting for their country chic wedding… check out some of the details… What is even more awesome about a lot of these items is that Jenna made a lot of them herself! She was a Superbride, and did I mention, she is an&nbsp;<a href="http://www.eventsyourwayatl.com/" target="_blank">event planner</a>?</p>
