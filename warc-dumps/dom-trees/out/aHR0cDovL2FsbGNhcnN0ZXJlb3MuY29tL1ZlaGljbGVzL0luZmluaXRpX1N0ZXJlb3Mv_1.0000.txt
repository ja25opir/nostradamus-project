	<h1>Stereos and Speakers for Infiniti </h1>

	
	<div style="float: right; padding: 5px; margin: 5px; border-width: 1px 1px 1px 1px; border-style: solid; border-color: white; background-color: white">
	
</div>

When you're hooking up a new stereo system in your Infiniti you'll need a number of components. First of all you'll need an amplifier (not a stock one). It really depends what you want to run in your ride. If you want to run some component speakers, and some subwoofers, you'll need an amplifier which meets your power requirements. Refer to the RMS value of each component and add it to find your total RMS watts required.<br><br>Is that your Infiniti sound system or is someone trapped in your trunk? Ever hear that line? No? You might, once you install your new Infiniti system. Just be sure to tune the sucker down a bit, especially if you're installing some loud subwoofers.
