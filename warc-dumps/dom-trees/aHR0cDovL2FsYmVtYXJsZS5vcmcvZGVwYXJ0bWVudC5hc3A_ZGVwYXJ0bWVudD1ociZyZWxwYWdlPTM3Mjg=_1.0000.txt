<p style="text-align: center"><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana">PLEASE HAVE ALL REQUIREMENTS COMPLETED AND WITH YOU AT ORIENTATION UNLESS OTHERWISE NOTED OR INSTRUCTED.</span></strong></p>
<ul>
    <li><em><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana"><a href="https://www.albemarle.org/applyforjob/">Online Application:</a></span></strong></em><span style="font-family: Verdana"> You must have an up-to-date job application on file with Albemarle County.&nbsp; This can be submitted from any computer with internet access or in the Human Resources Department during business hours.<br>
    &nbsp;</span></li>
    <li><em><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana"><a href="/upload/images/Forms_Center/Departments/Human_Resources/Forms/misc/Background_Investigation_Consent_Form.pdf">Criminal Background Consent Form</a>:&nbsp; </span></strong></em><span style="font-family: Verdana">In accordance with Virginia Code 22.1-296.2, all candidates for employment in the Albemarle County School Division are required to undergo an FBI fingerprint and criminal background check, as well as a Child Registry Search with the Virginia Department of Social Services.&nbsp; These will disclose convictions nationwide and must be completed before you begin working for Albemarle County.&nbsp; This can be done in the Human Resources Department in County Office Building, M-F, between 9am-4pm.&nbsp; No appointment is necessary.&nbsp; Please have the form completed when you come in for fingerprinting.&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>
    &nbsp;</span></li>
    <li><em><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana"><a href="/upload/images/Forms_Center/Departments/Human_Resources/Forms/misc/CPS_Form.pdf">Child Abuse Central Registry Check:</a></span></strong></em><span style="font-family: Verdana">&nbsp; If you print this form, it must be <strong>double-sided</strong> (1 sheet of paper with print on both sides).&nbsp; <strong>DO NOT SIGN</strong> the back of the Child Abuse Central Registry Check form until you are in the presence of a Notary Public.&nbsp; One will be available to you in HR at no additional charge.&nbsp; Do not leave any lines blank.&nbsp; Instead, put "N/A" (Not Applicable) on any line that does not apply to you.&nbsp; <strong>DO NOT MAIL</strong> this form as it directs on the sheet.&nbsp; HR will take care of this.&nbsp; Please make sure you have ID with you (ex: Driver's License) in order for the form to be notarized.&nbsp; The cost&nbsp;is included in the amount for the Criminal Background Screening.&nbsp; Forms are also available in HR.<br>
    &nbsp;</span></li>
    <li><em><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana"><a href="/upload/images/Forms_Center/Departments/Human_Resources/Forms/misc/Child_Abuse_State_Search_Acknowledgement_Form.pdf">Child Abuse State Search Acknowledgement:</a></span></strong></em><span style="font-family: Verdana">&nbsp; HR is required to conduct out-of-state searches for child abuse and neglect.&nbsp; Please list all states where you have lived during the past five years.&nbsp; HR will follow-up with you to collect additional information on those states if applicable.&nbsp;&nbsp; <br>
    &nbsp;</span></li>
    <li><em><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana">Tuberculin Screening:</span></strong></em><span style="font-family: Verdana">&nbsp; You must provide proof of a negative tuberculin test dated within the previous 12 months of your start date.&nbsp; If you have not had a TB&nbsp;test within the last 12 months, Albemarle County will cover the cost of the test for regular employees.&nbsp; Please contact HR for instructions and to pick up a form prior to testing.&nbsp; We require tht you provide your negative tuburculin results to us <strong>AT ORIENTATION</strong>&nbsp;in order to start working.&nbsp;&nbsp;New TB tests take <strong>48 hours</strong> to get the results so please plan accordingly.&nbsp; <br>
    &nbsp;</span></li>
    <li><em><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana">Employment Eligibility Verification:</span></strong></em><span style="font-family: Verdana">&nbsp; Please review the <strong><a href="http://www.uscis.gov/files/form/i-9.pdf">I-9 form</a></strong>, and come to Orientation&nbsp;prepared with one form of identification from List A <em><strong>OR</strong></em> one form from List B <em><strong>AND</strong></em> one form from List C.&nbsp; The list of acceptable documents is on the last page of the I-9 form.&nbsp; If you do not have the required documents, you will not be able to work until the form is completed by HR.&nbsp; <br>
    &nbsp;</span></li>
    <li><em><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana"><a href="/upload/images/Forms_Center/Departments/Human_Resources/Forms/New_Employee_Personal_Data_Sheet.pdf">New Employee Personal Data Sheet:</a></span></strong></em><span style="font-family: Verdana">&nbsp; This information is required in order to establish an employee record for you on our Human Resources and Payroll systems.<br>
    &nbsp;</span></li>
    <li><em><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana"><a href="http://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/fw4.pdf">W-4:</a></span></strong></em><span style="font-family: Verdana">&nbsp; Completion of the bottom of this form is necessary to claim any exemptions from federal income taxes.<br>
    &nbsp;</span></li>
    <li><em><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana"><a href="http://www.tax.virginia.gov/Web_PDFs/busforms/va4.pdf">VA-4:</a></span></strong></em><span style="font-family: Verdana">&nbsp; Completion of the bottom of this form is necessary to claim any exemptions from state income taxes.</span></li>
</ul>
<ul>
    <li><em><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana"><a href="/upload/images/Forms_Center/Departments/Human_Resources/Forms/Payroll/Direct_Deposit.pdf">Direct Deposit Form:</a></span></strong></em><span style="font-family: Verdana">&nbsp;&nbsp;Please complete this form and attach a <strong>VOIDED CHECK</strong> if you would like your pay to be automatically deposited into the financial institution of your choice, in lieu of a paper check.&nbsp; You will still receive a pay stub with an explanation of your earnings.&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>
    </span></li>
    <li><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana"><em><a href="/upload/images/Forms_Center/Departments/Human_Resources/Forms/Payroll/Pay_Option_Form.pdf">Pay Option Form:</a></em></span></strong><em><span style="font-family: Verdana"> <strong>(For employees starting at the beginning of the&nbsp;school year ONLY).</strong>&nbsp; </span></em><span style="font-family: Verdana"><em>10 and 11-month employees starting before September 15th, please choose the number of checks you would like as your pay option.&nbsp; If you do not complete this form, your pay will automatically be split so you receive the same number of paychecks as the number of months that you work in a year.</em></span></li>
</ul>
