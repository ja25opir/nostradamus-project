<p class="MsoNormal"><b>The motorcycling community nationwide has been notified

<p class="MsoNormal"><b>&nbsp;<o:p>
</o:p>
</b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>Your worthwhile effort was the subject of the worst kind
of bigotry promulgated by your own school officials, including your
superintendent and high school principal as well as some school board members.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;</span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>&nbsp;<o:p>
</o:p>
</b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>We know that your so-called community leaders would like
this incident to just go away.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>However,
hatred and bigotry, like so many other evils, never goes away.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;

heads.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>The editor of<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;

</span>He was wrong.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>The millions
in this country who ride motorcycles and know they are part of your American

</span>When you ignore stereotyping and bigotry you simply give it a chance to
grow and fester.</b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>&nbsp;<o:p>
</o:p>
</b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>We applaud you and think your poster was a creative and
unique effort to raise funds.</b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>We believe that you, as a team, have much to teach some
of your parents, school officials and school board members about acceptance,
inclusion and tolerance.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>They were
apparently trying to teach you the opposite.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
</span>A very poor role model, indeed.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>I
have enclosed a check for twenty dollars for your wrestling team, wish you the

forget what has happened in your community.<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp; </span>It may seem small now, but it isn’t.<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp; </span>One of our most precious rights is freedom of expression.
Another is freedom to associate.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>Some
in your town have forgotten some of the principals on which this country was
founded.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>I can only hope that you
never do.</b></p>
