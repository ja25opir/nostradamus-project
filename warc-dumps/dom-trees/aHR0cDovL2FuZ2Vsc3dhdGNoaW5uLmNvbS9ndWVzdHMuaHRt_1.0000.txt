		<p class="bodyText"><strong>Connecticut Bed and Breakfast</strong>. Angels Watch Inn, is located in Westbrook CT.  Come for a romantic weekend getaway.  Elope or have a small wedding. We offer Elopement and Small Wedding packages that are personal, intimate, romantic, stress free and very cost effective. Receive massage therapy, or one of our other wellness therapies.  Let us host your very own Murder Mystery Dinner for your next special occasion.  Angels' Watch Inn offers romance, privacy, dual person pedestal soaking tubs, fireplaces, canopied and sleigh beds, mid week and off season specials, and so much more! Just thirty minutes from Mystic CT.  Westbrook CT is a charming village situated along the Connecticut River Valley Shoreline.  We are just one and one half blocks from Long Island Sound.</p>
