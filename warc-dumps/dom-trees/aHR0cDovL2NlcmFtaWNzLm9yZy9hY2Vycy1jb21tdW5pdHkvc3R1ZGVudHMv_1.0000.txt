<p><strong>Get Involved in PCSA </strong></p>
<p>Do you have a keen interest in ceramics and strong leadership qualities? If so, play a big part in the future of the ceramics profession by getting involved in ACerS President’s Council of Student Advisors (PCSA). The PCSA is a student-led committee whose mission is to engage students as active and long-term leaders in the ceramics community and to increase participation in ACerS at the local and national levels. All PCSA delegates are nominated by their faculty advisors. Learn more about the <a href="?page_id=3018">PCSA’s goals and future plans</a>!</p>
<p><br class="spacer_"></p>
<p><br class="spacer_"></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Wondering What To Do After Graduation?</strong></span></p>
<p>ACerS can help you succeed by offering you a FREE Associate Membership for the first year following graduation. By becoming an ACerS Associate Member, you’ll have access to valuable resources that will benefit you now and throughout your career. Complete the membership application <a href="http://ceramics.org/associate-membership-application/" target="_blank">online</a> or <a href="http://ceramics.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/assoc_mem_application_bw_6910.pdf" target="_blank">download the application</a> and mail or fax it.</p>
