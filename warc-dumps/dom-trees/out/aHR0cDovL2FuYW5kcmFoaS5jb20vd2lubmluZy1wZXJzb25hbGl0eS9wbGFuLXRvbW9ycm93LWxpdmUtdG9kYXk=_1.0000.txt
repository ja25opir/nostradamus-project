                                    <h1 class="title">Plan  for Tomorrow But Live in Today</h1>
            <div class="tabs"></div>
                                    <!-- node --> 
<div id="node-5" class="node odd full-node node-type-blog">
		  	
  
  <div class="meta">
        <div class="submitted">Fri, 06/19/2009 - 06:38 — Anandrahi</div>
      </div>

    <div class="terms">
    <ul class="taxonomy"><li class="vocab-term"><a href="/category/categories/winning-personality" rel="tag" title="">Winning Personality</a></li></ul>  </div>
  
  
  <div class="content">
    <p>Animals don't make plan for future so they perish easily in a crisis, especially if they are not under protection of a human being.  If you don not plan for future you are sure to be enslaved by some superior powers in future. The more you use your vision for future the more freedom and prosperity you can enjoy. </p>
<p>Although you have to plan your future yet to live in the future is not wise.  In pursuit of the happenings of tomorrow we often lose joys hidden in today. Every nook and corner of today is filled with simple sparks of joy.  You need to have an open heart and open eyes. The life, then, can really be great fun. </p>
<p>If you really want to live a happy life the first lesson you need to learn is to live in today like children and teenagers live. Although our education system is so heavy on children and teenagers yet they know how to have fun of simple things in life.  </p>
<p>But as soon as they grow up more life becomes so burdensome that we are unable to move happily.  So change your attitude of living today, to have a better tomorrow. </p>
