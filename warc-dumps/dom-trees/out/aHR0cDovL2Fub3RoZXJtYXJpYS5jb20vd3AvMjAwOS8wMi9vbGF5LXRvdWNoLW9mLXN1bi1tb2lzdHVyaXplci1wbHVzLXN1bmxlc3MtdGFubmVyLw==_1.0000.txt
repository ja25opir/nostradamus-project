						<h2 class="posttitle"><a href="http://anothermaria.com/wp/2009/02/olay-touch-of-sun-moisturizer-plus-sunless-tanner/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent link to Olay Touch of Sun Moisturizer Plus Sunless Tanner">Olay Touch of Sun Moisturizer Plus Sunless Tanner</a></h2>
			
				<p class="postmeta"> 
				February 16, 2009 at 3:23 pm 
				· Filed under <a href="http://anothermaria.com/wp/category/health-beauty/" title="View all posts in Health &amp; Beauty" rel="category tag">Health &amp; Beauty</a>								</p>
			
				<div class="postentry">
									<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/guerra/3286244596/" title="Olay Touch of Sun by Another Maria, on Flickr"><img src="http://farm4.static.flickr.com/3642/3286244596_2a977d4e88_m.jpg" width="240" height="180" alt="Olay Touch of Sun" align="right" style="padding-left: 3px"></a>
</p><p>I have finally been able to use Olay Complete Touch of Sun (Daily UV facial moisturizer plus a touch of sunless tanner) a lot more since I have not tanned since January. I noticed that I have been getting PALE again and remembered I had this product so on some days I have been replacing my regular moisturizer in the morning with Olay Touch of Sun.
</p><p></p><h2 style="padding:0; margin:0">What I Love</h2>
<p>I was pleased to see that Touch of Sun offers SPF 15 UVA and UVB protection which is similar to the moisturizer I am currently using. This makes me glad because when using Touch of Sun I know I am not sacrificing anything for a bit of color!</p>
<p>I have tried sunless tanners that did not go on even and to make things worse the color was way darker than it needed to be – resulting in a self tanning nightmare! I purchased the lighter/medium tone and I like the results I am getting. No, it does not give me the same results as my regular tanning routine but it does the trick. I get a touch of color, similar to being in the California sun for a short while, without breaking a sweat! Remember, this product is intended for use on the face and neck and if the color change isn’t as dark as desired, simply apply Touch of Sun more frequently.</p>
<p>And don’t forget to exfoliate before applying any sunless self tanner for the best results.</p>
<p>View Olay’s Touch of Sun by <a href="http://www.olay.com/boutique/touchofsun/">clicking here</a>.</p>
<p>PS. I have not tried Olay’s Quench Plus Touch of Sun Body Lotion. I will definitely let you all know if I do in the future!</p>
