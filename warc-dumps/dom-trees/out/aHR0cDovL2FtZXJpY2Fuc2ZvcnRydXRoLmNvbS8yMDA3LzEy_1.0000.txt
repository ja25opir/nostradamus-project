				<h3 id="post-1696"><a href="http://americansfortruth.com/news/when-homosexual-on-homosexual-rape-is-called-a-hate-crime-the-felipe-rivera-case.html" rel="bookmark" title="When Homosexual-on-Homosexual Rape Is Called a ‘Hate Crime’: the Felipe Rivera Case">When Homosexual-on-Homosexual Rape Is Called a ‘Hate Crime’: the Felipe Rivera Case</a></h3>
				<small>Friday, December 28th, 2007</small>

				<div class="entry">
					<p>By Peter&nbsp;LaBarbera<strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p><strong>NOTE TO READERS:</strong> When we made our initial comments on our story, we were relying on what turned out to be a sloppy and erroneous <a href="http://www.chicagotribune.com/news/local/chi-hatearrest28_bothdec28,0,3702507.story?coll=chi_tab01_layout">Chicago Tribune story</a> (see bottom), and we had not seen the <a href="http://www.suntimes.com/news/metro/716770,CST-NWS-assault28.article">Chicago Sun Times</a> story on this crime, which conflicts with the Tribune account. I apologize for reporting this so quickly and for foolishly relying on a single Chicago media&nbsp;story for accurate news (not a good idea).</p>
<p>Here are the essential details from the <a href="http://www.suntimes.com/news/metro/716770,CST-NWS-assault28.article">Sun-Times article, “Alleged gay advance cited in rape”</a>&nbsp;(emphasis added):</p>
<blockquote><p><strong>Felipe Rivera</strong>, 43, is charged with a hate crime as well as aggravated criminal sexual assault and other offenses, said a spokesman for the Cook County state’s attorney’s office. If convicted, he could face more than 30 years in prison, a source said.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>According to a spokesman for Cicero police, Rivera and the victim encountered each other at a party Friday night in the 1200 block of South 50th Avenue.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>“Mr. Rivera got upset apparently because he believed the victim, No. 1, didn’t respond to a female and then, No. 2, somehow winked at him — made what he perceived as a sexual advance,” said Cicero police spokesman Dan Proft.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>30 years possible</strong><br>
Rivera then punched the victim in the face, Proft said, and was asked to leave the party. He allegedly waited outside for the victim. According to Rivera, the victim, 37, then propositioned Rivera for a sex act — a claim the victim denies, Proft said.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Rivera followed the victim to the outer staircase of a basement apartment, where he removed the victim’s pants and raped him, authorities said.</strong> Afterward, Rivera punched the victim in the head, leaving him semi-conscious, then inserted a metal broom handle in the victim’s rectum, sources said.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Rivera gave police these details in a videotaped statement, according to Proft. When asked why he did it, Rivera allegedly said it was “because he hates f – - – - – s, and this is what they get,” according to Proft.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>A spokesman for the state’s attorney said Rivera also shouted “sexually oriented derogatory comments” during the rape.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>After the alleged attack, Rivera went to his mother’s residence nearby. Because she has an order of protection against him, she called the police, Proft said.</p></blockquote>
<p>I just spoke with Cicero spokesman <strong>Dan Proft</strong>, who says that the <em>Sun-Times</em> story is the most accurate he’s read.</p>
<p>In our original commentary (below), I relied on the <em>Trib </em>account which appears to have mangled Proft’s&nbsp;statement so as to&nbsp;assert that the <em>violent assailant</em> propositioned his male victim for sex. According to the <em>Sun-Times</em>, however, the assailant claims that his <em>victim</em> did the propositioning (which the victim denies actually happened).&nbsp;</p>

				<h3 id="post-1691"><a href="http://americansfortruth.com/news/a-%e2%80%9cgay-man%e2%80%9d-trapped-in-a-woman%e2%80%99s-body-and-other-nonsense.html" rel="bookmark" title="A “Gay Man” Trapped in a Woman’s Body and Other Nonsense">A “Gay Man” Trapped in a Woman’s Body and Other Nonsense</a></h3>
				<small>Saturday, December 22nd, 2007</small>

				<div class="entry">
					<p><img id="image1690" style="height: 219px" height="219" alt="sistersfolsom2007_promo.jpg" src="http://americansfortruth.com/uploads/2007/12/sistersfolsom2007_promo.jpg" width="235">&nbsp;<em><strong>“Transgender” confusion and perversion — such as the anti-Christian, drag-queen mock “nuns”&nbsp;who make up&nbsp;the </strong></em><a href="http://www.thesisters.org"><em><strong>“Sisters of Perpetual Indulgence”</strong></em></a><em><strong> — follow in the footsteps of the homosexual variety.&nbsp;In Massachusetts, a&nbsp;very confused female student who considers herself&nbsp;a&nbsp;”gay male” is suing for the “right” to use the men’s&nbsp;locker room. The local&nbsp;newspaper uses the&nbsp;pronoun “his,”&nbsp;bowing to the female student’s (current) “gender identity.”</strong></em></p>
<p>By J. Matt Barber, <a href="http://www.cwfa.org/articles/14405/CFI/family/www.cwfa.org">Concerned Women for America</a></p>
<p>Talk radio host Michael Savage doesn’t mince words. He often says that “liberalism is a mental disorder.” He’s right. Liberal ideology and political correctness, infused with public policy, begets social insanity.</p>
<p>Case in point: A recent report on a popular homosexual “news” site declares, “A transgendered student says he is the victim of discrimination at a small Massachusetts community college <strong>because he is biologically female</strong>.”</p>
<p>Say what? “He” is “female”? Welcome to PC-ville. Come for the oxymoron, stay for the cognitive dissonance.</p>
<p>In other news, “French Emperor Napoleon Bonaparte says he is the victim of discrimination because he is biologically Bob from Detroit.”</p>
<p>Apparently, this clearly confused 20-year-old woman who “presents as a male and wears male clothing” is upset that <strong>Northern Essex Community College</strong> in Haverhill, Massachusetts, will not allow her to undress, shower or otherwise cavort with the fellows in the men’s locker room. She’s reportedly filed an “affirmative action complaint” against the school.</p>
<p>Even Haverhill’s local newspaper, the <em>Eagle-Tribune</em>, plays along, saying that she was “denied the use of the men’s locker room … because of his female anatomy.”</p>
<p>“His female anatomy”!?</p>
<p>Okay, wait a minute. … Is this one of those, “it depends on what the meaning of the word ‘his’ is” moments?</p>
<p>But it gets worse. <strong>The paper goes on to explain that this young woman – er, man … whatever – actually identifies as a “gay male.”</strong> It explains that her, “public unveiling as a transgender gay male happened as an adult two years ago. His designation means he has a female body, but identifies as a male and is sexually attracted to men.”</p>
<p>Geeze-oh-Pete! My head hurts. Can this be for real? How can any newspaper that wants to be taken seriously report this with a straight face? A “gay” man trapped in a woman’s body? And all this time I thought they were just straight gals in Dockers and flannel. When I was a kid we called them “tomboys.” They were great fun to play softball with, but they still had to shower with the rest of the girls. What unenlightened bigots we were back then. ….</p>
<p align="right"><a href="http://www.cwfa.org/articles/14405/CFI/family/www.cwfa.org">Click HERE to read the rest of Barber’s column, “A Gay Man Trapped in a Woman’s Body and Other Nonsense.”</a></p>
