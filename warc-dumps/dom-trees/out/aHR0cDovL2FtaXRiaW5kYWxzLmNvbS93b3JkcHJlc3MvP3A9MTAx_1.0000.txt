<h2>Getting you own Website</h2>

			<div class="date">
					2010 <br>
					02.17				</div>

				<div class="entry">
				<p><img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/banner-know-more.jpg" alt=""></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #333333;">You already passed first base – you have become aware of the need. Perhaps you saw the www. names and the @&nbsp;email addresses&nbsp;and wondered what they are all about, or you have been speaking with colleagues or competitors who have Websites, or read an article about how the web is going to change both society and business.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #333333;">Either way, you want to find out more about getting&nbsp;your business&nbsp;or organisation on the Internet. OK,This is how we do&nbsp;it.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/greendia.gif" alt="green diamond" width="14" height="14"> </span><strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#Forming Initial Ideas">Forming initial ideas</a></span></strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><br>
<img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/greendia.gif" border="0" alt="" width="14" height="14"> </span><strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#Checking The Details">Checking the details</a></span></strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><br>
<img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/greendia.gif" border="0" alt="" width="14" height="14"> </span><strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#The Draft Project Agreement">The draft Project Agreement</a></span></strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><br>
<img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/greendia.gif" border="0" alt="" width="14" height="14"> </span><strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#Registering Your Domain Name">Registering your domain name</a></span></strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><br>
<img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/greendia.gif" border="0" alt="" width="14" height="14"> </span><strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#The Final Stages">The final stages</a></span></strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><br>
<img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/greendia.gif" border="0" alt="" width="14" height="14"> </span><strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#Post Sales Support">Post-sales support</a></span></strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><br>
<img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/greendia.gif" border="0" alt="" width="14" height="14"> </span><strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#Future Developments">Future developments</a></span></strong></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#top"><span style="color: #333333;"><img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/top.gif" border="0" alt="up arrow" width="17" height="17"></span></a><span style="color: #333333;"> </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #333333;"><strong>Forming Initial Ideas<a name="Forming Initial Ideas"></a></strong><br>
One of the first issues you will need to consider, is what you want to achieve with&nbsp;your website, and whether you want to be able to sell directly from your web page and take money in real-time.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="color: #333333;">If you do, then see our section on e-commerce for more details about selling direct and taking money over the net.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;">Our&nbsp;Website Design&nbsp;Checklist raises a number of issues that you will want to consider, like what you want the website to do for you, how big you expect it to be, and so on.</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;">You will probably want to talk to your friends or colleagues about it. You might even want to check some points with us by phone or email, perhaps to get an idea how long it would take, and when we could start.</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;">Having assembled your initial thoughts, you will be able to complete the&nbsp;Website Design&nbsp;Checklist, then send it back to us.</span></p>
<p></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="color: #333333;">Remember, there is absolutely no commitment to engage us at this stage.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#top"><span style="color: #333333;"><img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/top.gif" border="0" alt="up arrow" width="17" height="17"></span></a><span style="color: #333333;"> </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #333333;"><strong>Checking The Details<a name="Checking The Details"></a></strong><br>
When we receive your completed Checklist, we will then arrange to discuss your site with you. Depending on your location, this can be in person, by phone, or by email.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="color: #333333;">This is where we go through your decisions and preferences from the&nbsp;Website Design&nbsp;Checklist, agree the number of pages, how they link to each other, and so on.</span></span></p>
<p></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="color: #333333;">We also make sure that we both understand what you want from your site, and decide some of the more technical matters – like checking what domain names are available; the initial site layout; and what the best file structure will be for the future.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#top"><span style="color: #333333;"><img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/top.gif" border="0" alt="up arrow" width="17" height="17"></span></a><span style="color: #333333;"> </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #333333;"><strong>The Draft Project Agreement<a name="The Draft Project Agreement"></a></strong><br>
At the end of this process we will be able to prepare a written project plan setting out all the points we have agreed, and advising you of the likely the cost of the work. Two copies of this will be sent to you, and if you want us to undertake the design, you are asked to sign and return them.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="color: #333333;">You will be asked to enclose a cheque/cash for 50% of the total project cost at this stage, and to begin to send us the text you want to use on each page, or at least a draft what you want to say, (preferably on a CD), together with any other materials that you want included (photographs etc.).</span></span></p>
<p></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="color: #333333;">We will sign and return your copy of the agreement. Returning the signed project agreement and part payment to us signifies your decision to engage us, and when you receive your copy, the agreement forms the contract between us.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#top"><span style="color: #333333;"><img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/top.gif" border="0" alt="up arrow" width="17" height="17"></span></a><span style="color: #333333;"> </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #333333;"><strong>Registering Your Domain Name<a name="Registering Your Domain Name"></a></strong><br>
The next stages are:</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"></span></p>
<ul type="disc">
<li><span style="color: #333333;">To&nbsp;register your domain name</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #333333;">To host the website.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="color: #333333;">Now we can begin work to build&nbsp;your website&nbsp;according to the Project Agreement.</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #333333;">When we have something prepared, you will be able to view the draft on screen. At this stage, we can incorporate minor design changes to suit your preference.</span></p>
<p></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="color: #333333;">You can also begin to plan changes to existing stationery (letterheads, invoices, brochures etc.) to include your new email and www addresses</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#top"><span style="color: #333333;"><img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/top.gif" border="0" alt="up arrow" width="17" height="17"></span></a><span style="color: #333333;"> </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #333333;"><strong>The Final Stages<a name="The Final Stages"></a></strong><br>
In the final stages,&nbsp;your Website&nbsp;construction becomes completed, and is tested off-line. Then we upload the pages to your service provider/website host &amp; test all the links to full&nbsp;working order.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #333333;">You approve the final site, and provide the final payment</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#top"><span style="color: #333333;"><img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/top.gif" border="0" alt="up arrow" width="17" height="17"></span></a><span style="color: #333333;"> </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #333333;"><strong>Post Sales Support<a name="Post Sales Support"></a></strong><br>
We troubleshoot any problems via telephone or email support for the next three months, and will check with you from time to time to see that everything is just as you would wish it.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="color: #333333;">During this time, any minor changes to your site, (perhaps a change of someone’s name, or phone number etc.) will be made without additional charge.</span></span></p>
<p></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="color: #333333;">New pages, or more significant alterations will be charged at the rate shown on the current price list.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><a href="http://amitbindals.com/how-do-i-get-one.htm#top"><span style="color: #333333;"><img src="http://amitbindals.com/images/content/top.gif" border="0" alt="up arrow" width="17" height="17"></span></a><span style="color: #333333;"> </span></span><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #333333;"><strong>Future Developments<a name="Future Developments"></a></strong><br>
We are of course, always available to extend, or further develop the site, or to provide other services in the future.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #333333;">Our aim is to exceed your expectations, and encourage you to suggest us to your colleagues and friends when they are considering having a website made.</span></p>
