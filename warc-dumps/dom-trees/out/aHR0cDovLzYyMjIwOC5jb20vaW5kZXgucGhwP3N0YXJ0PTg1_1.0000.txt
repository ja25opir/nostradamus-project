It was not a very pleasant road, as they were busy fixing its numerous potholes and closed off the one lane.&nbsp; This meant that there were queues waiting for oncoming traffic which caused some delays. There was nowhere to stop, so we covered the first 150kms in one go.<br><br>On our way to the halfway town of Karibib, we passed a girl cyclist on a heavily loaded bicycle struggling her way in the Namibian heat and wind.&nbsp; We remarked that she is carrying more luggage than the both of us together.&nbsp; After shopping and ice cream at the Karibib OK, we covered the rest of the way.<br><br>30km from the Spitzkoppen, you turn onto a dirt road towards the campsite.&nbsp; Although the road was in good condition, it was a bit scary to feel how unstable it is with all the extra load.
