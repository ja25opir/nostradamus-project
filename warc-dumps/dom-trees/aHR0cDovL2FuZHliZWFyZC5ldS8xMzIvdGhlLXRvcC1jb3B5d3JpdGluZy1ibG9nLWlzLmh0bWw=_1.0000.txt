    			<h1 class="entry-title">The Top Copywriting Blog is…</h1>
<div class="entry-meta"><span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">By </span><span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://andybeard.eu/author/Andy Beard/" title="View all posts by Andy Beard">Andy Beard</a></span><span class="meta-sep meta-sep-entry-date"> | </span><span class="meta-prep meta-prep-entry-date">Published: </span><span class="entry-date"><abbr class="published" title="2006-11-14T20:16:15+0000">November 14, 2006</abbr></span></div><!-- .entry-meta -->
				<div class="entry-content">

<p>I came across something a few days ago, and I have been itching to let the cat out of the bag.</p>
<p>Lots of bloggers would immediately think of the excellent <a href="http://www.copyblogger.com">Copyblogger</a>, who has <a href="http://www.copyblogger.com/copyblogger-joins-b5-media/">just announced joining B5 Media</a>.<br>
Brian gets a significant amount of traffic to his blog, and has 6k+ loyal readers.</p>
<p>But I things are going to maybe get a little more competitive in the very near future.</p>
<p>I am on a fair number of email lists, and I haven’t seen mention of this blog yet. I certainly haven’t seen mention of it on the creators own blog…</p>
<p>Do any of these names ring a bell?</p>
<p>    * Alan Forrest Smith<br>
    * Andy Catsimanes<br>
    * Gary Halbert<br>
    * John Anghelache<br>
    * John Ritz<br>
    * Michael Morgan<br>
    * Michel Fortin<br>
    * Ray Edwards<br>
    * Shawn Catsimanes<br>
    * Troy White<br>
    * Zachary Romero</p>
<p>Every single one of them is a well known copywriter. Obviously it isn’t a comprehensive list… yet.</p>
<p>I have a feeling the line-up will grow fairly rapidly.</p>
<p>The new site is aptly named <a href="http://www.copywritersblog.com">The Copywriters Blog</a> and is the brainchild of <a href="http://www.michelfortin.com/">Michel Fortin</a>.</p>
<p>Many of the writers in the initial lineup frequent Michel’s popular <a href="http://www.copywritersboard.com">Copywriting forum</a>.</p>
<p>From Day 1 (well it isn’t really day one yet, as I haven’t seen it announced), they have a professional looking blog that is highly organised. There are a few pagerank leaks I would shut the door on, such as those to Technorati, but otherwise it is a fairly tight ship, and makes good use of tagging, categories and related posts.</p>
<p>One interesting aspect is that this blog is produced by multiple authors with authority. You can expect the content to be widely syndicated on sites such as Google News sometime in the future.</p>
<p><strong>My prediction for the future</strong></p>
<p>Expect great things of <a href="http://www.copywritersblog.com">The Copywriters Blog</a>, as I think it is going become a resource to be reckoned with.
</p><div id="active-share-comment-marker"></div>
