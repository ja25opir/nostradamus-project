<p class="MsoNormal" align="center" style="text-align: center; margin-right: -.25in">
<span style="font-family: Arial; font-weight: 700"><font size="4">"The 
Solicitor"</font></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" align="center" style="margin-right:-.25in;text-align:center">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">by</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" align="center" style="margin-right:-.25in;text-align:center">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">Andrew Olson</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">When I call, it’s usually after 
5:00 p.m.—but never later than 7:00. I always ask for the person I’m calling for 
by the first name. Is Jill there? Is Mark there? And then, if they’re still on 
the line, I talk to them about what I’m selling. Oh, don’t be mistaken—most 
people hang up on me. Probably seventy-five percent. Another five percent listen 
for 30 seconds, and then hang up. Another ten percent listen for a minute, and 
nine percent for a minute and a half. A measly one percent make it to the end. 
That’s when I lay it on them, when they find out this call isn’t like any 
ordinary solicitor calling.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The reason most 
people I hang up, I think, is they don’t believe me. If I was on the other end 
of the line, I'd certainly question what I was saying. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The one person out 
of a 100 who stays on the line usually thanks me from the bottom of his heart. 
It’s not uncommon for their families to send me cards. This makes perfect sense, 
because the families are the ones who benefit the most from my calls.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Then there’s the 
occasional person who doesn’t believe me. Like the other day. The man I called 
wanted to toy with me, like I was someone asking for money. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Hello, is this 
Steven?” I asked him.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “No, it’s Joe Bob. 
Who the hell is calling?”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “My name is Stanley 
Morgan, from Morgan Associates—”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Oh shit, another 
solicitor. Oh, Christ, you’re the fifth to call me tonight!”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “I want you to 
listen very carefully to what I have to say, Steven. I’m only going to say this 
once. Are you listening?”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Oh, I’m listening 
like you can’t believe, Stanley Morgan.” And he laughed.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “This isn’t a 
joke.” I cleared my throat. This is always the hardest part. “Steven, you’re 
going to die tomorrow. I know this is difficult, but this is your last chance to 
help out your family. This is your last chance to buy life insurance, to take 
care of your wife, Nancy, and daughter Eva.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Silence on the line.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Steven are you 
there?”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “This is the 
funniest thing I’ve heard all day—no, funniest thing all year. Do you wait till 
the holidays to call people like me?”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “As I’ve already 
told you, this isn’t a joke.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “You keep telling 
that, but what kind of credentials do you got? Are you God or something?”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Hardly, Steven. 
I’m just trying to help you out.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Well, even if I 
did believe you, what the hell are you gonna do now? If I’m dying tomorrow, how 
am I going to sign up for some friggin’ insurance policy?”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Have you got 
today’s mail, Steven?”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “The mail? Sure, I 
got it. It came with a stack of bills as high as my knee. I don’t look through 
that shit till the middle of the month.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “I want you to go 
to your stack of mail. You should see a letter from Morgan Associates.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; I heard Steven drop 
the phone and go look for the letter. I could hear him grumbling as he looked 
around. He managed to insult the cat, the dog, even his daughter—and finally 
came back to the phone.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Yeah, I got it. 
What do you want me to do with this?”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Open it up. You’ll 
see two pieces of paper. In order to qualify for&nbsp; $1 million of Life Insurance for 
your family, just check the box on the first piece of paper.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “What box?” Steven 
asked.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; I realized I was 
dealing with someone who probably couldn’t read.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “There’s only one 
box, Steven.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Hey, don’t get all 
condescending with me!?”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Just check the box 
and sign it. Use the return envelop to send the first piece of paper back to me. 
Have you checked the box and signed the piece of paper, Steven?”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Look, I don’t know 
what this is. I mean, you could be asking me to sign away my life.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Steven, it’s very 
important you do this. It’s the difference between your family being comfortable 
in the next few years—and having serious financial problems.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “I don’t think I 
want to sign it, Mr. Morgan. Something smells fishy here.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “What do you have 
to lose, Steven? And even if you like, go ahead and read through the document. 
I’ll wait. It’s written in plain English. Go ahead. I’ll be right here.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Steven read the 
document. It was only 12 words long, and the box was very simple:</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;</span></p>
<blockquote>
	<ul type="circle">
		<li>
		<p class="MsoBlockText">
		<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">Check here to receive 
		$1 million life insurance. Guaranteed. No questions 
	asked.</span></p></li>
	</ul>
</blockquote>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Look, why don’t I 
call you back, Mr. Morgan.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “There’s no time 
for that, Steven. You either sign and send off that document tonight, or your 
family has a lot of problems.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “You sound like 
you’re threatening me now. Doesn’t make me really want to sign anything.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Look, Steven, it 
goes like this: I could call 1000 people tonight, but I’ve only got time and 
resources to call five people. You’re one of the lucky five.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Or unlucky,” he 
said.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “The point is, you 
have this tremendous opportunity to give your family the security they need. You 
can give them the life they deserve. Isn’t that what you want?”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Well, of course it 
is.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Then please sign 
the form and put it in the mail.”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
<span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; “Is this some kind 
of practical joke?”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 24.0pt">
