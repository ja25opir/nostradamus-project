		<h2>The Only FREE Bootcamp That Will Really Teach You REAL MLM Secrets!</h2>
		<div class="byline">January 29th, 2010 by Aaron Chen</div>
		<div class="entry">
						<div id="greet_block"><noscript><div class="greet_block"><div class="greet_text"><div class="greet_image"><a href="http://aaronchenonline.com/feed/rss" rel="nofollow"><img src="http://aaronchenonline.com/wp-content/plugins/wp-greet-box/images/rss_icon.png" alt="WP Greet Box icon"></a></div>Hello there! If you are new here, you might want to <a href="http://aaronchenonline.com/feed/rss" rel="nofollow"><strong>subscribe to the RSS feed</strong></a> for updates on this topic.<div style="clear:both"></div></div></div></noscript></div><div style="float:left;margin-right:10px;"><iframe height="69" width="53" src="http://widgets.fbshare.me/files/fbshare.php?url=http://aaronchenonline.com/mlm-marketing-training/freebootcamp&amp;title=The Only FREE Bootcamp That Will Really Teach You REAL MLM Secrets!&amp;size=large" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowtransparency="true"></iframe></div><p>You know when I first started in this business, I didn’t realize how many things you had to learn yourself and apply if you wanted to be successful. But when I did finally realize that, I went out and bought EVERYTHING!! I spent tens of thousands of dollars on my personal education, and you know what, some training was good, and some was crap! The good thing though, is I did learn from most of them and was able to really push my marketing and skills to the next level.</p>
<p>The only thing I thought when I was going through all this training, was that I haven’t really seen any FREE training out there that goes into as much depth as some of these paid trainings do. And for good reason I suppose. But then the other side of the story is where you get tons of paid training that turns out to be garbage and so difficult to follow that you wonder why you paid $300 for it in the first place. I’m sure you know exactly what I’m talking about.</p>
<p>So what I decided to do was to give back to the community. And what I’ve done is created my own training series that will help the newbie network marketer to get their feet wet and really understand what works and what doesn’t when they are growing their MLM business online. Now this isn’t just a series of autoresponders that talks about marketing on each different day.</p>
<p>No no….this is a full on 5 day bootcamp that gives you video instructions on how to set up your social sites, how to leverage them properly so that you get maximum value.&nbsp; It also teaches you how to generate leads effectively instead of wasting 10 hours on Facebook everyday with nothing to show for it. I go into keyword research and how to find the best keywords for all your marketing campaigns and I even show you how to come up with great content if you’re not that experienced to create your own yet.</p>
<p>I don’t stop there though. I take your hand and walk you step by step through the process of setting up your article sites and how to find the best sites with the highest PR so that your articles rank better. Then I teach you how to PROMOTE all the valuable content you just created to the entire planet so that people will notice your work.</p>
<p>Now I could charge for this. And I should. But i’m not going to. Well, not for now anyway…. <img src="http://aaronchenonline.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_wink.gif" alt=";)" class="wp-smiley"> </p>
<p>So all you have to do to get access to this awesome bootcamp that is going to sit you down and spoon feed you for the next 5 days, is put your name and email address into the boxes on the right hand side of this post and press the “FREE Access Now” button! &gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;&gt;</p>
<p>Now you’re rocking!</p>
<p>Sit back, relax and really take in all this information. Because you’ll probably never find it anywhere else.</p>
<p>I hope you enjoy it as much as I enjoyed putting it together.</p>
