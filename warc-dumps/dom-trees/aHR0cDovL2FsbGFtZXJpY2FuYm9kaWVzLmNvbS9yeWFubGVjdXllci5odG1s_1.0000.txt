                        <br>Ryan L'Ecuyer is a 19 year old upcoming bodybuilder from Connecticut.  He stands 5'10" tall 
                            and weighs 176 lbs. in competition.  He has a 32" waist with a 46" chest.  Ryan had an 
                            active sports background, wrestling in school before realizing that he was much more
                            passionate about bodybuilding.  He realized the two sports did not mix, and so is now 
                            pursuing his bodybuilding dream.  He started training in a gym at age 11, and got more 
                            serious about bodybuilding at 14.  To date, he has been very successful in bodybuilding 
                            competitions, including the 2009 USBF Empire States Natural (2nd Place Teen Class, 1st 
                            Place Novice Lightweight Class), the 2010 INBF Natural Connecticut (1st Place Teen Class, 
                            2nd Place Open Middleweight Class) and the 2010 INBF Hercules Championships (1st Place 
                            Teen Class, 2nd Place Open Middleweight Class).  We met Ryan competing at the 2010 
                            Hercules show, and with the help of NAB photographer Steve Roberts, we photographed him 
                            during the course of that show.  We hope to schedule a photoshoot with Ryan L'Ecuyer again 
                            in the future, and for now we are pleased to introduce him to you and add Ryan to our 
                            roster of upcoming bodybuilders and fitness models at NorthAmericanbodies.com!<br>
