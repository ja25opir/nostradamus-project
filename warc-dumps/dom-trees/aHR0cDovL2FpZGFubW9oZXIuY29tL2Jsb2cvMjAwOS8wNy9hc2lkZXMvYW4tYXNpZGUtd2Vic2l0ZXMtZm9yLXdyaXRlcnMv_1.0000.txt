        <h1><a href="http://aidanmoher.com/blog/2009/07/asides/an-aside-websites-for-writers/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link: An Aside | Websites for Writers">An Aside | Websites for Writers</a></h1>
		<span class="post-cat"><a href="http://aidanmoher.com/blog/category/asides/" title="View all posts in Asides" rel="category tag">Asides</a>  <span class="post-print"><a href="http://aidanmoher.com/blog/2009/07/asides/an-aside-websites-for-writers/print/" title="Print This Post" rel="nofollow">Print This Post</a>
</span></span> <span class="post-calendar">July 3rd, 2009</span>
		<div class="post-content">

		<div id="content_div-1229">
<p>If you’re anything like me (and I’d guess you are, since you’re reading a web site about books and writing!), you’re probably always on the prowl for new quality web sites about writing and reading. Well, thanks to Shaun at <a href="http://wisb.blogspot.com/">The World in a Satin Bag</a>, I found a web site that does all the dirty work for us!</p>
<p><img src="http://aidanmoher.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/07/wsfw-logo.png" alt="Websites for Writers Logo" title="Websites for Writers Logo" width="400" height="77" class="center"></p>
<div class="quote">
<p><strong>About</strong></p>
<p>Websites for writers is an independent directory of online writing resources.
</p><p>The internet is littered with useful websites aimed at writers of all shapes and sizes. Websites for writers showcases the best of those sites to celebrate their wonderfulness and point writers in the right direction.</p>
<p>You can subscribe to the RSS feed or get regular updates via email for free by submitting your address using the form opposite.</p>
<p><strong>Get involved</strong></p>
<p>This directory is a collaborative effort. It’s not one long list of unmoderated links. The sites featured here are chosen by professional, practising writers. It’s designed to grow and evolve, not sit and stagnate.</p>
<p>And you can help. If you think a site should be included on Websites for writers, let us know through our submission page.</p>
</div>
<p>It’s a new website, and still in the early stages of growing, but already it’s chock full of some great sites for readers and writers. I’ve submitted <strong>A Dribble of Ink</strong> (which I hope will be added!), and even found a few new websites for perusal. </p>
<p>You can find Websites for Writers <a href="http://www.websitesforwriters.net/">HERE</a>. So why not head on over and check it out, even submit your site if you have one and it applies. It’s sure to be a great resource in the future.</p>
