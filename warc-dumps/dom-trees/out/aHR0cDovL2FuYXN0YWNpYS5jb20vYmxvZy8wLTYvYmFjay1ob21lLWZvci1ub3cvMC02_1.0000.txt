            	                  <h4>Back home.... for now</h4>
                    <h5>Tue 24 Aug, 2010</h5>
                    <p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">Hi Fan-ily</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">Ohhhhh Boy, what a work trip I have had over these past six weeks!!! I am still buzzing with happiness as I reflect back on this time.....</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">I am at the airport awaiting my flight back to LA and wanted to say hello to everyone!!!</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">I had such a great time being a judge on the TV show and hope there will be another one in the future.....</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">I won't be home for long though.....((-:</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">I will be heading back to the UK to do some promo in Sept for Here Come The Girls!!!!</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">Yesssss, it is indeed true, the Diva's are heading back to do another round of UK dates!!!! Lulu and I are so blessed to have a new sista to work with, Heather Small who is amazing!!!!! </p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">We will surely have a great show with us three ladies!!!</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">I can't wait to see familiar faces again!!! It was a treat and a tease to see so many of you at the portugal gig....I hope I will see a bunch of you in Athens for the Eurovoice show!!!! As well as perhaps the Cypress show....((((-:</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">I adore all of you and thank you again for filling my heart with your unconditional love and support!!!</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoPlainText" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt">xoAnastacia</p>
