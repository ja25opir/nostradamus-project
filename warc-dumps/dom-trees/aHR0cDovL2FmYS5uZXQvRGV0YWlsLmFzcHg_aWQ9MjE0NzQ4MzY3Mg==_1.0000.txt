	<h2>Our&nbsp;grandchildren and great-grandchildren will be paying off the debt we are incurring</h2>
<span class="xc_maintext"><h4><span class="xc_maintext">January 14, 2009</span></h4>
</span><p><span class="xc_maintext"><strong>Our government now owes more money than all of us in the country put together possess. We now owe nearly $57 trillion while our net worth is $56.5 trillion.</strong></span></p><div><br><strong>President-elect Barack Obama has said that we can expect a trillion dollars in additional debt each year as far as the eye can see into the future.</strong> Soon Obama will ask Congress for an additional trillion dollars to bail out certain large companies.</div><div><br>Remember when they gave $350 billion to the banks? That money is gone and guess what? <strong>The banks cannot or will not tell us what they did with it.</strong></div><div><br>Obama's plan, being promoted by Democrats and Republicans alike, is to pass out money, running up debt that our grandchildren will be forced to pay. Their solution is simple. Throw more money at the problem.</div><div><br>Where will the government get these trillions of dollars? They will go to the printing press and print more, <strong><font color="#0000ff">which means that dollar in your pocket will be worth less</font></strong>.</div><div><p><strong><em>What's a trillion dollars? </em></strong></p>
<p><em>A trillion dollars = $1,000,000,000,000.</em></p>
<p><em>That's 12 zeroes to the left of the decimal point. A trillion is a million million dollars.</em></p>
<p><em>The U.S. government spends more than the entire Gross Domestic Product (GDP) of Australia, China and Spain combined. If you laid one dollar bills end to end, you could make a chain that stretches from earth to the moon and back again 200 times before you ran out of dollar bills! </em></p>
<p><em>One trillion dollars would stretch nearly from the earth to the sun. It would take a military jet flying at the speed of sound, reeling out a roll of dollar bills behind it, 14 years before it reeled out one trillion dollar bills.</em></p>
<p><em>American workers now net almost 30 percent less in real wages than they did in 1973. After taxes, two paychecks in a family barely equal the purchasing power one had thirty years ago</em>.</p>
<p>It also means the government will borrow more. From whom? Try China and the rich oil producing countries. At the rate we are going, China will never have to fight us. They will own us.</p>
<p>Our leaders, if that is what you want to call them, are unwilling to call us to bite the bullet and pay the price for our irresponsible financial behavior. <strong>It is immoral to pass this debt on to our grandchildren.</strong></p>
</div><div><br>The time to act is now, before it is too late. <strong>While it may be painful now, failure to act will be deadly in the future.</strong></div><div><br><span style="COLOR: #993300"><strong><a title="Take Action" href="http://capwiz.com/afanet/issues/alert/?alertid=12434536&amp;type=CO">Take Action</a></strong></span></div><ul>
<li>Enter your Zip Code and hit "GO" to send an e-mail to your representative and senators telling them not to support the trillion dollar bailout. </li>
<li>Please forward this e-mail to family and friends. If Congress fails to hear from their constituents they will probably vote for Obama's trillion dollar bailout.</li>
</ul>
