				<td content csheight="290" width="289" height="303" colspan="5" rowspan="15" valign="top" xpos="178">
					<p>The Kwik-Lathe is the industry’s first on-the-car lathe with over 30,000 units sold. Bolting the lathe on the car in place of the caliper allows the operator to accurately machine the rotor as it relates to the car’s spindle and hub assembly. Guaranteed “Zero Runout”. The Kwik-Lathe is equipped with common and special adapters to handle a wide variety of wheels. Lathe includes a “Kwik-Mount” fixture for fast and accurate set-up and an instructional video.</p>
					<ul>
						<li>Machine rotors on the vehicle, guaranteed zero runout. Eliminate pedal pulsation, costly comebacks, and unhappy customers.
						</li><li>Kwik-Mount fixture makes set-up and alignment fast and accurate.
						</li><li>Money saved by resolving customer complaints and reducing comebacks pays for the machine in one year.
					</li></ul>

				<td content csheight="244" width="291" height="259" colspan="7" rowspan="3" valign="top" xpos="176"><font size="2" color="#ff0033"><b>Power Drive</b></font><br>
					The Kwik-Way Power Drive greatly enhances the versatility and quality of your on-the-car brake work. It provides turning power to the rotor, enabling you to machine rotors not powered by the vehicle's drive train. The constant speed of the power drive promotes a consistent finish. The need to raise and lower wheels, engage gears and vent exhaust fumes are all eliminated with the Power Drive, saving time and money.
					<ul>
						<li>Consistent rotor finish alignment fast and accurate.
						</li><li>Improved versatility
						</li><li>No exhaust fumes
						</li><li>Increased productivity
