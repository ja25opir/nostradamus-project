<h3>March 18th</h3><div id="node-976" class="node">


  <h2><a href="/blog/training-starts-today" title="training starts today!">training starts today!</a></h2>

  <div class="content">
    <p>Now that VLDB insanities, and St. Patrick’s Day’s festivities are behind us, I begin my uphill climb towards getting rid of all the evil life-shortening substances I have put in my body over the last few months. The goal is to not embarrass myself at the <a href="http://www.dexterannarborrun.com/tenk.htm">Dexter-Ann Arbor 10K run</a>, which is on the 1st of June. I have 2 months and 12 days to do this; so hopefully this isn’t an impossible task.</p>
<p>The plan is to detox and switch to a strictly healthy diet first. So dear friends, if you ever see me eating anything that says “McDonalds” or “Milky Way” on it, please feel free to slap me in the face and shake some sense back into me. That’s what friends are for, after all. The second plan of attack is to start running Monday, Wednesday, Fridays along with strength exercises, and go swimming Tuesdays, Thursdays, Saturdays. Sunday is rest day. For March, let’s keep running at 3 miles a day, and 500m swimming. Thankfully I’ve been doing 60 push-ups everyday already, and have been running once in a while, so my body should not collapse by April. </p>
<p>To be honest, I’m not looking forward to the sugar and fried food cravings. But I’ll do anything for <a href="http://www.dexterannarborrun.com/shirt.htm">a T-shirt</a>, and this should be worth the trouble. Expect weekly updates on this front!</p>  </div>

<h3>March 11th</h3><div id="node-974" class="node">


  <h2><a href="/blog/ventage" title="ventage">ventage</a></h2>

  <div class="content">
    <p><em>(lyrics in progress, mostly to prove i ‘nuj i can spew spite on demand. don’t worry, i’m perfectly ok :) )</em></p>
<p>Tongue-twisted, flabbergasted<br>
Frustrated, no control<br>
mind afire, shattered pieces<br>
a bruised and battered soul</p>
<p>that’s what you did<br>
you killed the flame<br>
you slit the veins, now<br>
you take the blame</p>
<p>my deeds are not my doing<br>
if you consider this<br>
my death was by your thoughts<br>
and your affected bliss</p>
<p>i create, you consume<br>
you repress, i resume<br>
i extinguish, you exhume<br>
yet you profess that i presume?</p>
<p>you dress like you’re perfect<br>
you drink like you’re flawed<br>
you spend every breath in<br>
a yearning for applause</p>
