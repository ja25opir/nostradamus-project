<p align="center"></p><h1 class="title" align="left"><font size="4">欧盟“纾困希腊”承诺未平息市场担心</font></h1>
<p class="title" align="left">　欧盟向危机中的希腊提供支持的承诺，刺破了金融市场对快速纾困的希望，引发市场担心抛盘再度出现。<br>　　A pledge by the European Union to stand by crisis-hit Greece punctured hopes in financial markets of a swift rescue and raised fears of renewed selling.</p>
<p class="title" align="left">　　法国、德国领导人尼古拉·萨科齐(Nicolas Sarkozy)和安格拉·默克尔(Angela Merkel)努力掩盖两国之间关于如何处理希腊债务危机的分歧，导致在布鲁塞尔举行的欧盟首脑会议声明中，未宣布立即对希腊提供援助。<br>　　Efforts by Nicolas Sarkozy and Angela Merkel, the French and German leaders, to paper over differences between Berlin and Paris on how to deal with the Greek debt crisis led to a summit statement in Brussels that stopped short of providing immediate support for Athens.</p>
<p class="title" align="left"><br>　　欧盟27国领导人承诺，“如有需要，将采取坚定和协调行动，维护欧元区的稳定”。由于担心希腊的债务危机可能扩散，债券市场出现了大幅动荡。<br>　　The leaders of the 27-nation bloc promised “determined and co-ordinated action if needed to safeguard stability” of the eurozone, which has been shaken by turmoil in bond markets amid fears that Greece's debt problems could spread.</p>
<p class="title" align="left"><br>　　该承诺相当于隐含保证，如果希腊在4、5月份为其债务再融资出现问题，将提供帮助。<br>　　The accord amounted to an implicit assurance to help Athens if it had problems in refinancing debt in April and May.</p>
<p class="title" align="left"><br>　　但是，由于声明中缺乏详细的救援计划，金融市场投资者感到失望。虽然全球股票和债券市场均小幅上涨，但欧元兑美元仍下跌0.9%。<br>　　But the lack of a detailed bail-out plan underwhelmed investors in financial markets. While global stocks and bonds made modest gains, the euro fell 0.9 per cent against the dollar.</p>
<p class="title" align="left"><br>　　欧盟常设主席赫尔曼·范龙佩(Herman van Rompuy)称，这一承诺乃是一项“政治声明”。他表示，欧盟领导人没有提出详细救援方案，是因为希腊还没有要求提供援助。<br>　　Herman van Rompuy, the EU's permanent president, described the deal as a “political statement” saying leaders had not come up with a detailed rescue package because Greece had not asked for assistance.</p>
<p class="title" align="left"><br>　　萨科齐说：“我们致力于展示欧盟的团结、透明度和纪律。”<br>　　Mr Sarkozy said: “We have committed to a show of solidarity, transparency and discipline.”</p>
<p class="title" align="left"><br>　　欧盟同意了希腊将今年的预算赤字削减（占国内生产总值）4个百分点的计划。<br>　　The EU endorsed Greece's plans to cut its budget deficit by 4 percentage points of gross domestic product this year.</p>
<p class="title" align="left"><br>　　声明规定了一系列政策准则，如果希腊希望获得金融援助，它将需要遵循这些准则。金融援助最有可能在未来某个阶段来自德国、法国和其它一些欧元区国家。欧盟期待雅典将“采取一切必要行动，包括采取更多措施”，以求到2012年消除赤字。</p>
<p class="title" align="left">　　The agreement lays out a set of guidelines that Greece will be expected to follow if it were to be given financial support, most probably from Germany, France and some other eurozone countries, at some stage in the future. Athens would be expected to “do whatever is necessary including adopting additional measures” to eliminate the deficit by 2012.</p><p></p></div>
