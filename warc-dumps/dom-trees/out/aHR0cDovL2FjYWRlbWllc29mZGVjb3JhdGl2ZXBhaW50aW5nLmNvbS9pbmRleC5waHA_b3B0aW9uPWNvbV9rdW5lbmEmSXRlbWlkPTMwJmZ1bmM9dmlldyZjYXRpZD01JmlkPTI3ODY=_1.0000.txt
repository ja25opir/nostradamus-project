I was just thinking about the Rosemaling seminar and noticed that David had posted this thread.  It was an amazing event.  I absolutely love Rosemaling...the history, the opportunity to move it in a new direction, the elegance of the scrolls and flowers.  I also have come to value the experiences of painting in groups.  It inspires me and helps me grow as an artist and as a person.  In this particular class there were people that were "doing their own thing" as they made Rosemaling fit in to their own artistic termperment. In my particular case I painted the lesson as instructed but also designed and painted another smaller piece of my own design so that I could cement the style and come away with a few original pieces.
