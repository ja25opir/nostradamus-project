					<td bgcolor="F4E9E9" class="text" style="padding-left:15; padding-right:10">Counterbalance fork truck: Purpose of use <br>
It is a popular equipment having an extensive use in the heavy engineering industry. The tons of loads are to be carried to a far-flung corner within the boundary of an organization. This object also lifts the heavy loads and carefully places them on the platform. It is a heavy item and requires a goodly sum to invest into. But the monstrous strength to bear the heavy weights of the pallets as well as quick and efficient functioning have made the counterbalance fork truck a part and parcel of the industry. <br>
Counterbalance fork truck: Follow a few tips prior to buying <br>
While investing a huge bulk, always make sure that your investment does not get wasted. If you have ended up buying a faulty object, then you do not have any other way but to leave them at one corner or lock them into a room. Obviously, as an entrepreneur, you always aim at optimizing the resources and do not like any mishap happen to you. Keeping in mind the value of your hard-earned money, here is a handful of tips that you should follow prior to buying the counterbalance fork truck. <br>
The prime consideration before buying the truck is obviously its load capacity. The truck has to travel a lot with an excessive amount of load. So a counterbalance fork truck should be strong enough not to collapse down under pressure. This truck comes in various formats and the load capacity expectedly varies with its size. Another consideration is the placement of the fork. It is one of the vital parts of the counterbalance fork truck. The lifting ability of the truck is dependent upon this item and any fault in the fitting of the fork will turn out to be troublesome in the future. <br>
The counterbalance fork truck needs to be immune to the explosion and it must conform to some stringent rules as laid down by the ATEX 94/9/EC directive. The capacity of raising the loads up to the desired height is also an important factor to take into consideration. The height requirement varies from one sector to another, therefore one should concentrate on buying the item that meets his requirements and not waste money on purchasing the truck that can lift the loads to more-than-required height. Your meticulous disposition prior to purchasing any item may irritate the sellers. Not only in case of the fork trucks, but you should also show the same degree of cautiousness while purchasing the pallet trucks, lifts or any other engineering equipments.   <br>
The inching pedals should be placed at a considerable distance from the brakes. The impact quotient is another important aspect. Avoid buying the product having a very high quotient as it may cause damage to the other major components of the counterbalance fork truck. The floor flatness has immense importance as it determines how smoothly it can carry the load. Follow these tips to a T and make a good investment into a counterbalance fork truck. <br>
