<center><b><font face="Verdana"><font size="+1">Melancons gather in Opelousas</font></font></b><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2"></font></font>
<p><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
By PHILLIP de VALCOURT, <b>Daily World, 8/15/99</b></font></font></p></center>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody></table></center>
<font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
The organizers of the Melancon family reunion held at the Yambilee Building
in</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Opelousas Saturday had the ambitious goal of trying to trace family members
back</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
to the two Melancon brothers who arrived in Nova Scotia in 1657.</font></font><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2"></font></font>
<p><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
"Pierre and Charles arrived in Nova Scotia in 1657 and married Acadian
girls there</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
and that was the beginning of the Melancon family in North America," Linwood</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Melancon of Breaux Bridge, an organizer of the reunion said.</font></font><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2"></font></font>
</p><p><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
"We're trying to create a family tree to bring everybody back to the two
original</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Melancon brothers," Melancon said.</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
In the genealogy room set up at the Yambilee Building, organizers had deportation</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
documentation and Cajun roots on computer screens, videos about the exile
of the</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Acadians from Nova Scotia and records, including an Acadian census from
1671 to</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
1752, for interested Melancons to peruse and study.</font></font><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2"></font></font>
</p><p><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Caroline Turner Ogle, president of the "Les Famille de Melançon,
Melanson," helped</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
people, with the aid of a computer program, to position themselves in the
Melancon</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
family tree.</font></font><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2"></font></font>
</p><p><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
"We're putting a book together that will be an extended version of one
already put</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
out in Canada on the Melancon family," Ogle said.</font></font><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2"></font></font>
</p><p><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
With what Melancon estimated as about 20 Canadians and people from Texas,</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Florida, South Caroline, Illinois, Georgia, Mississippi, Alabama and other
areas of the</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
country, the reunion approached the goal of being a "world family reunion."</font></font><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2"></font></font>
</p><p><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
"We tried to invite Melancons from all over to come and visit to show people
their</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
family roots," Melancon said. "We also want to promote the Cajun culture
since so</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
much of it gets lost. If we have things like this, we hope to keep it alive."</font></font><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2"></font></font>
</p><p><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Melancon estimated about 500 people had attended the reunion as of 1 p.m.&nbsp;</font></font><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2"></font></font>
</p><p><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
One of those was Charles Melancon Jr. from Covington who said he had taken
a</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
tour of Nova Scotia last year and was fascinated by meeting other Melancons
there.</font></font><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2"></font></font>
</p><p><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
"We're all related to the two original brothers," Charles Melancon Jr.
said. "I'm here</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
just to see my cousins."</font></font><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2"></font></font>
</p><p><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Linwood Melancon said that this was the first year they had attempted a
reunion and</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
hopes to continue the practice in the future and perhaps get even more
Melancons</font></font>
<br><font face="Verdana"><font size="-2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
together.&nbsp;</font></font></p></td>
