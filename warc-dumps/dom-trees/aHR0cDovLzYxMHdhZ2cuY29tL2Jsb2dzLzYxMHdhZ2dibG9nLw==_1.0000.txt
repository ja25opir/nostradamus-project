The Hawkins name will be forever sketched into the history of gospel music--particularly the ground-breaking sound we now enjoy. He earned nine Grammy Award nominations and won one in 1980. The Bishop won many Stellar Awards and was inducted into the Christian Music Hall of Fame in 2007.
