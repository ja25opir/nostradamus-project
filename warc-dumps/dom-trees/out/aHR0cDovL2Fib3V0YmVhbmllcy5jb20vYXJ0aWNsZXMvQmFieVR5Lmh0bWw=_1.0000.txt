<p>
<a href="../library.html"><img src="../images/babyty/BabyTy_Banner.jpg" width="582" height="54" alt="BabyTy" vspace="5" border="0"></a>
<br>
<font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
<font color="blue"><b>
HISTORY:
</b></font>
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">

BabyTy was introduced Tuesday, January 4th, 2000 on the Ty.com website. The BabyTy line was created to replace the Pillow Pals. Both lines were created for young children.
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
BabyTy products are soft and made for cuddling! They have no sharp edges are parts that could potentially fall off or become hazardous to young children. All BabyTy have a rattle inside of them, perfect for a Baby! 
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
All BabyTy are made with Ty's patented fabric, Tylon. Each BabyTy is approximately 13" inches tall and are all produced with soothing pastel colors! 
 The left side of each BabyTy is an embroidered Ty heart logo.
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
The first BabyTy retirement occurred on June 26th 2000 quietly on the Ty Retailers website, 
<a href="http://www.ty-retailers.com" target="parent">http://www.ty-retailers.com</a>. 
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
Visit the Official BabyTy website <a href="http://www.ty.com/cgi-bin/osform/prodaux?osform_template=tyfamily_js.oft&amp;ProdLineName=Baby%20Ty" target="parent">here</a>!
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
<font color="blue"><b>
MATERIAL CHANGE:
</b></font>
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
<img src="../images/babyty/BabyTy_Tag_Tush_Tyhair_Front.jpg" align="right">
On November 1, 2001 Ty introduced four new BabyTy! However, these four new little guys are very special because they are no longer made with Tylon, they are made with a new Ty patented material called Tyhair! These little guys are even softer than their counterparts! 
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
This new style still contains the rattle, however they look more like the old Pillow Pals collection, they are flatter in shape, unlike the plump roundness of the first BabyTy. 
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
<font color="blue"><b>
PRICE:
</b></font>
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">

The suggested retail price for BabyTy is $10 US.
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
<font color="blue"><b>
TAGS:
</b></font>
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">

There are two generation of BabyTy tags:
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
<font color="blue">1ST GENERATION:</font>
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
<img src="../images/babyty/BabyTy_Tag_Front.jpg" align="left">
All BabyTy hang tags look like Beanie Baby tags, they open like a locket, and are the same size! They look a lot like 3rd generation Beanie Baby Tags, with the large "Ty" font! The BabyTy tags also have the word "Baby" written in Blocks! This first generation tag had the Babies name written inside on the right in the center of the tag, with the Ty website underneth. The inside left of the tag said "The Pillow Pals Collection," followed by a list of the 4 Ty headquarters, ©Ty Inc., ©Ty Canada, ©Ty Europe, and ©Ty Japan, with "Made in China" written underneth.
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
All first generation hang tags also have the following Tush tag with Tylon:
</font></p><p><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
</font></p><center><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
<img src="../images/babyty/BabyTy_Tag_Tush_Tylon_Front.jpg">
<img src="../images/babyty/BabyTy_Tag_Tush_Tylon_Back.jpg">
</font></center><font size="3" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
The fact that these BabyTy still said "The Pillow Pals Collection," inside confirmed what was thought, that BabyTy replaces the Pillow Pals. It was thought that someday Ty would print "BabyTy" on the tags, however to this day that still has not yet happen!
<p>
<font color="blue">2ND GENERATION:</font>
</p><p>
<img src="../images/babyty/BabyTy_Tag_Inside.jpg" align="right">
In January of 2001 several new BabyTy were introduced! Along with the introductions came new Tags! These tags again looked more like the 3rd generation Beanie Baby tags! They now have a spot inside on the right to write TO and FROM! Other than that the tag remained the same!
</p><p>
The first BabyTy's with 2nd Generation tags had the Tylon tush tags above, it wasn't until November 1, 2001 that BabyTy started appearing with the following Tyhair tush tags:
</p><p>
</p><center>
<img src="../images/babyty/BabyTy_Tag_Tush_Tyhair_Front.jpg">
<img src="../images/babyty/BabyTy_Tag_Tush_Tyhair_Back.jpg">
</center>
