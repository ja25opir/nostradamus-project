<div class="articletext">
<p>With Internet popularity at it's highest in history, starting your own home based business has never been easier.  People from all over the world access the Internet everyday surfing, shopping and searching for information.  This gives you the opportunity to capture customers in almost any area of business you choose.  While there are literally hundreds of potential businesses you could start, the best business in my opinion is Affiliate Marketing.  </p>
<p>With a basic set of computer skills and the ability to learn a few new ones, you have the potential to earn a very large income online.  But don't get fooled by it's simplicity, Affiliate marketing is no get rich quick scheme.  As with any business, you need to have a business plan and follow it to build your business to survive long term.</p>
<p>To help you, I have compiled a list of tips that will help you avoid some of the potential mistakes people make and allow you to build you business as quickly as possible.   </p>
<p>1.  Take your time</p>
<p>I know you are anxious to get started but the biggest mistake you could make would be to get started before you are ready.  Your website is only a small part of setting up an Affiliate Marketing business but, as with every part of your business, it needs to be set up properly.  Take your time and make sure you follow your business plan.  Don't rush any part of the process as this may lead to you missing small details that could cause problems in the future.</p>
<p>2.  Invest in an Affiliate Marketing course</p>
<p>Even if you have a little experience, I recommend you invest in a good Affiliate Marketing course.  There are many good ones online that will teach you each step you need to take to set up your business.  As you learn each step of the course, implement what you have learned in your own business. This will take some extra time to set up your business but in the long run it will be worth it.  An investment like this is an investment in yourself and is a very wise investment for your long term success.</p>
<p>3.  Don't fall for the hype</p>
<p>As you spend more time online searching for ways to increase your knowledge of the business, you will undoubtedly come across people promising things that may seem too good to be true.  Don't fall for this hype.  If someone tells you you can make money in no time with no effort, it's a bold faced lie.  If you begin to fall for the notion that you can make a quick killing online, you will probably find yourself jumping from one junk product to the next.  Don't be that person.  Build yourself a real business the slow and steady way and forget all of that other hype garbage.</p>
<p>4.  Build an email list </p>
<p>Because most people who visit your website will not buy anything let alone come back to visit again, you need to design a way to get people to sign up to be on your email list.  This will give you the ability to form a bond of trust with them over a period of time.  When you are ready to start collecting information, sign up to a reputable autoresponder service and give yourself more chances to convert those visitors into paying customers.</p>
<p>5.  In the beginning, stay away from PPC</p>
<p>Pay per click or PPC advertising is a difficult and sometimes costly skill to master.  You could literally waste hundreds or even thousands of dollars before you truly understand what you are doing.  So before you try your hand at PPC, make some money first and then invest in a PPC course before you jump in blindly.  There are people online who do nothing but keep track of all of the changes going on with PPC and teach other people the newest techniques to advertise successfully.</p>
<p>An Affiliate Marketing business is truly one of the best home businesses you can start.  Although it will take some time and energy to get it going, it is certainly much easier and faster than most other business out there.  If you take the time to learn a few new skills and invest the necessary time and sometimes money, the rewards could be extremely large.  Use the tips I've given you as a starting point.  One important thing to remember is that you can learn everything there is to know about Affiliate marketing but if you don't apply what you learn to your business it will never go anywhere.  If you do, there is literally no limit on how much money you could make.</p>
