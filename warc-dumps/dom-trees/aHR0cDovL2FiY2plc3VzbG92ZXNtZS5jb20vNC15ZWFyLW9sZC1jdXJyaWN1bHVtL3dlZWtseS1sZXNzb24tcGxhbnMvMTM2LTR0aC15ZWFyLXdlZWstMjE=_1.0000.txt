Have the child cover their eyes while you remove one of the objects.&nbsp; After uncovering their eyes, have them decide which item is missing.&nbsp; Start with a few items and gradually increase the difficulty.&nbsp; (<em>Idea from </em><a href="http://members.tripod.com/~preschoolresource/august2.html" target="_blank" title="Tripod"><em>Tripod</em></a>)<br>- Blindfold your child seated on the floor.&nbsp; Place four or five objects in front of him.&nbsp; Ask for a particular object.&nbsp; Although he may be able to feel for the object, having&nbsp;similar objects makes it more difficult.&nbsp; Discuss that some people cannot see, and that we should be thankful for our eyesight.&nbsp; <br>- Play <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000EGZ7PI?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=abjelome-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B000EGZ7PI" target="_blank" title="Pin the Tail on the Donkey"><font color="#0000ff"><u>Pin the Tail on the Donkey</u></font></a> <br><br>•&nbsp;Smell <br>Discuss: What part of our body do we use to smell? Talk about the nose and the job it performs. You may also wish to&nbsp;discuss how animals use their noses to find food.&nbsp;&nbsp;(<em>Idea from </em><a href="http://members.tripod.com/~preschoolresource/august2.html" target="_blank" title="Tripod"><em>Tripod</em></a>)<br><br>Activity:&nbsp; Using different items in your refrigerator, pantry, and bathroom, discuss the smell using words like sour, sweet, etc.&nbsp; Have your child close&nbsp;her eyes or use a blindfold and guess the item that&nbsp;she smells (lemon juice, milk, applesauce, rubbing alcohol, mouthwash, etc.).<br><br>•&nbsp;<a href="http://www.myyoungchild.org/index.htm" target="_blank" title="My Young Child"><font color="#0000ff"><u>My Young Child</u></font></a> Character Education Story - read online for free or print.&nbsp; Be sure to do the discussion and activities for each book.<br>&nbsp; - <a href="http://www.myyoungchild.org/stories/rosa_learns_forgiveness/index.htm" target="_blank" title="Rosa Learns Forgiveness"><font color="#0000ff"><u>Rosa Learns Forgiveness</u></font></a></p><p>•&nbsp;View &amp; Read:<br>&nbsp; <img src="/images/amazon/Forgivethem" border="0" alt="God Wants Me to Forgive Them" title="God Wants Me to Forgive Them" width="53" height="75">&nbsp; <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0001WTWW4?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=abjelome-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B0001WTWW4" target="_blank" title="VeggieTales - God Wants Me to Forgive Them?"><font color="#0000ff"><u>Veggie Tales: &nbsp;God Wants Me to Forgive Them?</u></font></a><br><img src="/images/amazon/has" border="0" alt="Wonderful Wizard of Ha's" title="Wonderful Wizard of Ha's" width="75"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000TUDFE4?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=abjelome-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B000TUDFE4" target="_blank" title="Veggie Tales: The Wonderful Wizard of Ha's"><font color="#0000ff"><u>Veggie Tales: The Wonderful Wizard of Ha's</u></font></a><font color="#0000ff"><img src="http://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=abjelome-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B000TUDFE4" border="0" width="1" height="1">&nbsp;<br></font><img src="/images/amazon/goldenrule" border="0" alt="Berenstain Bears and the Gold Rule" title="Berenstain Bears and the Gold Rule" width="75" height="75">&nbsp; <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0310712475?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=abjelome-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0310712475" target="_blank" title="The Berenstain Bears and the Golden Rule"><font color="#0000ff"><u>The Berenstain Bears and the Golden Rule</u></font></a></p><p>• <a href="http://www.starfall.com/n/holiday/calendar/load.htm?f&amp;n=main" target="_blank" title="Calendar"><font color="#0000ff"><u>Calendar Activity</u></font></a>&nbsp;- Here is an activity that would be a great start to each day to reinforce numbers, days of the week, and months of the year.&nbsp; </p><p>• Play the "Prodigal Son" adventure and use the coloring sheets on the <a href="http://www.christianbook.com/Christian/Books/product?event=AFF&amp;p=1152765&amp;item_no=78847" target="_blank" title="Charlie Church Mouse Kindergarten CD-Rom"><font color="#0000ff"><u>Charlie Church Mouse&nbsp;Kindergarten CD-Rom</u></font></a>.&nbsp;
