</script></div><p>Modern technology, owes ecology, an apology is a very famous old saying about the great advancements in technology that do good to the mankind but destroy the valuable natural resources. If you look around you, you can see that whenever, there is a technological advancement, there will be an ecological problem associated with. So obviously, the old saying is becoming very much true enough. Things aren’t very different in the case of the web hosting technologies.</p>
<p>Unfortunately, technological advancements are very rapid in the case of web hosting and the damage to the environment is greatly increasing each day. Apart from the carbon footprints that the systems of web hosting emit each day, the world’s greatest natural resources are being exploited for web hosting today. You will be well aware of the fact that the most advanced web hosting technologies requires very powerful servers to run on and so these powerful servers require huge energy to operate. </p>
<p>As the demand for web hosting increases, the energy consumption by the service providers have reached its peak. If this situation prevails, we will just end up in power crisis. It would be great if someday, the web hosting service providers find solutions for their power consumption. The day is not so far. It is closer than we think. The green web hosting solutions have already started to gain huge popularity worldwide.</p>
<p>Green power For Green Technology</p>
<p>Making use of green power is the only solution to make the web hosting solutions become more eco-friendly. Power consumption is not the only harm that the modern web hosting poses to the environment. When compared to the energy consumption all other harms such as small carbon foot prints are just negligible. Thankfully there are great alternative options to generate green power to run the power hungry servers. Some of the very best green web hosting service providers makes use of the solar energy and wind energy to generate the electricity they need. This method of green hosting is more eco-friendly as they don’t need any energy backup systems that have harmful emissions.</p>
<p>Many people have misconception that the green energy sources such as the solar power and wind energy cannot produce necessary energy to run the advanced servers 24/7. The real fact is that these green energy sources can run servers which support advanced Ruby on Rails Hosting and Joomla web hosting too. Though most of the Ruby on Rails Hosting and Joomla web hosting service providers have not adopted the green web hosting methods, the use of alternate power sources is slowly gaining popularity. Because of the reason that the maintenance and running costs of green energy solutions are less when compared to the conventional sources, the web hosting service providers are happy to adopt it.</p>
<p>Happy or not happy- If green hosting is not a future technology, our future in this planet will be a question mark. </p>
