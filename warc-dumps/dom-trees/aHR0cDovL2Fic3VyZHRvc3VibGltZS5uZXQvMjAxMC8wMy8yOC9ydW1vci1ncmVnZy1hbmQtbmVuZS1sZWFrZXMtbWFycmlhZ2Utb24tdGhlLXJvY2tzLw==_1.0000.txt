				<h4>&nbsp;</h4>
<p><img title="<p>NeNe Leakes</p>" src="http://www.radaronline.com/sites/default/files/photos/image_20100323/181894.jpg" alt="DNP Random Things"></p>
<blockquote><p><em>Real Housewives Of Atlanta</em> diva <strong>NeNe Leakes</strong> has been ‘reading her son <strong>Bryson Bryant</strong> the riot act’ after he got busted on a drugs charge and the arrest has put pressure on her own marriage, <a href="http://www.radaronline.com/exclusives/2010/03/real-housewife-nene-leakes-marriage-under-pressure-following-sons-drug-arrest">RadarOnline.com</a> can reveal.</p>
<p>Bryant was arrested on Sunday, March 7, 2010, while visiting an inmate at the Gwinnett County Jail in Atlanta – authorities found marijuana on the 20-year-old while arresting him for failing to appear in court for a traffic violation.</p>
<p>He was released several days later after posting bond but the incident has caused a lot of friction inside the Leakes’ <a href="http://www.radaronline.com/exclusives/2010/03/real-housewife-nene-leakes-marriage-under-pressure-following-sons-drug-arrest#">household</a>.</p>

<script type="text/javascript" src="http://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/show_ads.js">
</script></div><p>A source told RadarOnline.com: “The arrest really upset NeNe as she knows her son is hanging out with a bad crowd after he dropped out of University and started working as a club promoter in Atlanta.</p>
<p>“It has put pressure on her relationship with her husband Greg Leakes who is not Bryson’s real father as they both know that he’s still drinking and smoking with his buddies.</p>
<p>“NeNe perceives herself as a real socialite in Atlanta and she does not like the fact that Bryson’s arrest generated bad publicity for her and she is worried that he might get into more trouble in the future.</p></blockquote>
